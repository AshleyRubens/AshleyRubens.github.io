About Us

Welcome to Sweet Cheeks Baking Company

Founders Mary Odson and Lisa Ritter openedSweet Cheeks Baking Company in 2006 with the desire to become a friendly neighborhood bakery specializing in American-style baked goods and custom cakes.

We continue the tradition today by offering childhood favorites and custom cake designs which we bake from scratch using the finest ingredients. We still crack cage-free eggs, use pure vanilla extract, imported chocolate from Belgium and France and yes, you most definitely taste the difference.

In 2014, we opened a second store in downtown Los Angeles. We have an incredible team of cake artists whose designs are frequently displayed on our Instagram page.
